# AI Startup Discovery Lab

## Mission

AI Startup Discovery Lab is a structured research and validation initiative focused on identifying startup-grade opportunities that can evolve into:

* Final-Year Engineering Projects
* Smart India Hackathon Solutions
* Research Publications
* Commercial SaaS Products
* Venture-Backed Startups

The objective is not to generate generic project ideas but to systematically discover opportunities with strong technical depth, market demand, startup potential, and recruiter value.

# Evaluation Framework

Every opportunity must be evaluated against:

## Business Metrics

* Market Opportunity
* Customer Demand
* Revenue Potential
* Startup Feasibility

## Technical Metrics

* Engineering Complexity
* Research Novelty
* AI Depth
* Scalability

## Practical Metrics

* Dataset Availability
* Development Cost
* Team Feasibility
* 15-Month Achievability

## Career Metrics

* Recruiter Value
* Portfolio Strength
* Industry Relevance
* Skill Development

---

# Opportunity Rejection Criteria

Reject opportunities that:

* Are common student projects
* Are easily found on YouTube
* Are generic Kaggle projects
* Require enterprise-scale budgets
* Require inaccessible datasets
* Have no clear customer
* Lack differentiation
* Depend on unrealistic assumptions

---

# Final Decision Framework

A project should only be selected if it satisfies all of the following:

* Strong market demand
* Real customer pain point
* Technical innovation
* Research contribution
* Recruiter impact
* Startup potential
* Feasible for 3 engineers
* Achievable within 15 months

Projects that fail multiple criteria should be eliminated regardless of technical attractiveness.

# Opportunity Space Research

## Purpose

This document captures the major opportunity spaces identified during AI Startup Discovery Lab research.

The goal is to identify industries and technology intersections with:

* Growing demand
* Strong startup potential
* Research opportunities
* High recruiter value
* Real-world impact

---

# Opportunity Space 1

## AI Agent Security & Runtime Governance

### Domains

* AI
* Cybersecurity
* Cloud
* Agentic AI

### Problem

Organizations are rapidly deploying autonomous AI agents without sufficient visibility, governance, or security controls.

### Key Areas

* Agent Identity Management
* Agent Access Control
* Runtime Monitoring
* Prompt Injection Detection
* Tool Usage Analysis
* Behavioral Anomaly Detection

### Why Now

The adoption of AI agents is growing faster than security solutions can adapt.

### Market Signal

One of the strongest emerging cybersecurity categories between 2025–2026.

# Opportunity Space 2

### Domains

* DevSecOps

* Dataset Integrity

# Initial Research Conclusion

The strongest convergence of:

* Innovation
* Startup Potential
* Research Novelty
* Recruiter Value
* Technical Feasibility

was found in:

1. AI Agent Security & Runtime Governance
2. AI-Native Software Supply Chain Security
3. Shadow AI Governance

These areas became the primary focus for deeper analysis in subsequent research stages.
